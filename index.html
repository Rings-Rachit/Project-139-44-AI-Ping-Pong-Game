AI Ping Pong







Instructions

  1. Keep your face around 2-3 feet away from your camera.
  2. You will be using your right wrist to move your tile. Raise your wrist until a red dot appears on it - this will now control your vertical movement in the game.
  3. Start Playing by pressing the Play button... (duh)
  4. The red paddle on the left is yours. The other is the game's AI playing against you.
  5. The ball's speed will increase after every hit, making the game progressively harder. Try to beat my high score of Speed 10. Good luck! - Rachit S.
